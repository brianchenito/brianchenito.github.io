About

Hi, I'm Brian Chen, cyclist, gamer, 3D artist. I am a graduate of the eWaves 3ds Max Professionals Program, and currently a freshmen at University of California, Merced, studying Computer Science and Engineering in hopes of soon becoming a technical artist. Through submissions to the Dota 2 Community Workshop, I have familiarized myself with every stage of the character art pipeline, and have taken projects from concept to final product.

I am a fast learner, and familiar with 3ds Max, Zbrush, Maya, Photoshop,and Unity, with some knowledge of Javascript, HTML, and Git, as well as a host of supplementary programs such as xNormal and Hammer Editor.
